Crystals Collectors!


You will be given a random target number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on each you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to random number, you loose the game if your total scores go above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden form you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
